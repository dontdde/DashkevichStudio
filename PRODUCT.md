# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Founders, small business owners, operators, and teams who need a practical partner for launching or improving digital products. They are often comparing small studios and freelance teams, looking for clarity, technical confidence, and a low-friction first conversation.

## Product Purpose
Dashkevich Studio is a lightweight marketing site for a small development studio. It presents two primary directions: full-cycle product development and AI agents/automation. The site should convert early visitors into qualified leads through Telegram, email, and a simple project form.

## Brand Personality
Precise, calm, and technically awake. The studio should feel small enough to be personal, but structured enough to handle real product work across frontend, backend, integrations, AI automation, launch, and support.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic agency templates, oversized SaaS hero cliches, vague "we build digital experiences" copy, noisy gradients, fake metrics, complicated backend/admin promises, and pages that look like an AI-generated landing page. The MVP should not feel like a marketplace, a corporate consultancy, or a personal resume.

## Design Principles
- Show full-cycle capability without sounding inflated.
- Make the two directions obvious: product development and AI agents.
- Keep the first contact path immediate and low friction.
- Use concrete service language instead of abstract marketing claims.
- Let technical confidence come from structure, examples, and restraint.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA for contrast, keyboard navigation, focus states, semantic HTML, and readable Russian copy. Respect reduced motion. Do not rely on color alone to convey status or category.
